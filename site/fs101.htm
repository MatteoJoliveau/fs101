Who is a part of the Hive Mind at Fight Scenes 101? What do they know about writing action?

Primary Writer: Rach
Rach has been writing fights and action for much of the last fifteen years. Her fandom credits include Highlander, Power Rangers and Queen of Swords, including a stint of writing battles for one of the online RPGs in Highlander.

Assistant Writers: Gamine and Terri
Gamine is an accomplished author in Power Rangers and Pirates of the Caribbean. Terri is an accomplished writer in Buffy as well as being a font of knowledge big and small when it comes to the Jossverse. Both enabled Rach to avoid glaring errors and provided numerous insights which helped in putting this project together.

Testers: Jonathan and Mandolin
Veterans of the world of anime, Power Rangers, and Buffy amongst others, both Jonathan and Mandolin kindly donated time and energy to this project by testing, checking, correcting and trying these essays.
